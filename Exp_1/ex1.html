Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU)

Pandit Deendayal Energy University (PDEU), located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, India, is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education in the fields of engineering, technology, and management. Established in 2007, PDEU is recognized for its focus on sustainable energy, innovation, and research, making it a key player in the development of India's energy sector. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines, including petroleum engineering, civil engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science. PDEU is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, dedicated faculty, and a strong emphasis on hands-on learning and industry collaborations. In addition to its academic rigor, PDEU is committed to fostering an environment of research and development. The university has established several research centers focusing on renewable energy, energy management, and other key areas in the energy sector. PDEU also encourages entrepreneurship and innovation, offering platforms for students to develop and showcase their ideas. With a strong alumni network and partnerships with various industries, PDEU provides students with ample opportunities for internships, placements, and real-world exposure. The university continues to evolve as a center of excellence, contributing significantly to the education and development of professionals who will drive India's future in energy and technology.

Schools and Departments

  • School of Technology (SoT)

    Offers programs in Civil, Mechanical, ICT, and Computer Engineering, focusing on practical learning and industry collaboration.

  • School of Energy Technology (SoET)

    Specializes in Chemical, Electrical, and Petroleum Engineering with emphasis on sustainable energy solutions.

  • School of Management (SoM)

    Provides MBA programs in Energy, Infrastructure, and General Management, integrating business acumen with energy expertise.

  • School of Liberal Studies (SLS)

    Offers diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs fostering creativity, critical thinking, and interdisciplinary learning.
